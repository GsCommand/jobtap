The problem with quoting off a guess

Quoting sq-ft work off eyeballing a curb means you're either scaring off the customer with too high a number, or eating the loss when the driveway turns out bigger than you thought. Walking it with a tape measure works — but it means a second trip before you can even send a price.

How JobTap does it

Measurement flowing into a quote

Type the address, the satellite view loads over the property, and you tap the corners of the driveway, pool deck, or patio. The square footage updates live as you go. Inside the real app, that number drops straight into the quote builder — no re-typing, no second guess.

What fires automatically vs. what you tap

Fires automatically

  • Satellite imagery loads once you find the address
  • Square footage recalculates live as you trace
  • Saved measurements stay on the property for next time

You tap

  • Type or confirm the address
  • Tap each corner of the area
  • Undo, clear, or save the trace into a quote

Questions

Do I need an account to try this?

No. This demo runs with no login. Saving a measurement to a real client and quote requires a JobTap account.

How accurate is it?

It's a satellite trace — accurate enough to build a fair quote, not a substitute for a legal survey.

What if the address doesn't center correctly?

Pan and zoom the map by hand and trace the driveway or pool deck you can see — the sq-ft math doesn't care how you got there.
