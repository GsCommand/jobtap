Missed call

Your phone rings while you're on a roof or running a hose across a driveway. JobTap catches the miss and knows a lead just walked in the door.

Lead

A text goes out before they hang up and call the next guy. They reply, and it's a live lead sitting in your list — not a voicemail you'll listen to at 9pm.

Quote

Build a quote by square footage or flat rate right from your phone, then text it over with a tap-to-approve link — no printer, no PDF email.

Job

An approved quote lands on the calendar as a job. Head out and an on-the-way text fires the moment you tap it — the customer knows you're coming.

Invoice

Mark the job done and the invoice goes out automatically. Get paid by card, tap-to-pay, or however you already collect.

Paid

The second an invoice is marked paid, the follow-up year starts on its own — Day 3, 30, 60, 90, 180, 270, 365. You don't have to remember any of it.

Review

Three days after the job, a review request goes out with a direct link to Google — with click tracking so you know it actually landed.

Repeat

The annual reseal reminder fires on schedule, and Customers Due shows you who's coming up — so repeat business happens without a spreadsheet.
